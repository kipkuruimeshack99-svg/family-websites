Services Offered

Our family website provides the following services for family members, students, and the community:

  • Member Directory: Keep track of all family members and their contact details.
  • Student Support: Access resources and information for students in school or tertiary institutions.
  • Employment Assistance: Connect job seekers with family employers or local opportunities.
  • Networking: Facilitate networking among family members for social and professional purposes.
  • Announcements: Share family news, events, and achievements with all members.
  • Contact & Support: Provide an easy way for members to contact administrators for help or inquiries.
